# Zero-to-one portfolio headline

## Goal

Make Anjelika's strength in creating new products and capabilities visible immediately, while preserving the evidence that she also carries them through launch and live operation.

## Approved hero structure

- Role eyebrow: `Product Manager`
- Primary headline: `I take complex B2B2C products from 0 to 1.`
- Supporting message: explain that Anjelika turns ambiguous client and operational needs into testable product capabilities, then follows them through engineering, QA, release and live use.

The supporting message must not repeat the headline or turn it into a list of PM buzzwords.

## Evidence boundary

The zero-to-one claim is supported by:

- Custom Workflows, which progressed from initial actions into an operable automation platform;
- new identity, integration and bulk-operation capabilities created from client needs;
- the multi-region loyalty programme workstream from data and product rules through launch and operation; and
- the AI-ready product-memory practice built for requirements, QA, research, onboarding and handover.

Store remains an evolution and platform-expansion story rather than being recast as wholly greenfield work.

## Scope

Update the homepage hero headline and its supporting copy. Reflect zero-to-one in SEO and the resume summary only where it reads naturally. Keep `Product Manager` as the visible professional title everywhere. Do not change the approved case-study hierarchy or inflate individual ownership.

## Acceptance criteria

- The homepage H1 uses the approved sentence exactly.
- `Product Manager` remains the visible title.
- Supporting copy connects zero-to-one work to engineering, QA, launch and live operation.
- The portfolio does not imply that every case began from zero.
- The resume remains one page and ATS readable if its summary changes.
- Existing evidence qualifications, confidentiality and collaborator attribution remain intact.
